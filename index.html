BEATSLOTS

Enemies march on the beat. Kill them on the beat to steal pieces of music. Each piece fills one of your 8 slots — the metronome walks the rack, one slot per tick, playing your song as it grows. Press E on the beat to fire the ability of whatever slot the playhead is on: kicks build platforms, bass launches you, snares recharge your dash, leads supercharge your gun. Rearrange the rack to sequence your abilities. Reach the flag.
